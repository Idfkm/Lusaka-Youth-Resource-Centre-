Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about LYRC

LYRC offers youth-focused programmes across practical learning areas, mentorship, and skills development pathways. We offer over 50 training opportunities and learning tracks.

You can apply online through our Admission Portal, or visit the Admissions Office. The application deadline is typically June 15. You'll need SAT/ACT scores, high school transcripts, and a personal essay.

Yes, LYRC offers on-campus housing for students. Contact us for more information on housing.

Most programmes are 3 months. Depending on the programme. Some accelerated options are available.

LYRC programmes are quality-assured and delivered with recognized partners and professional standards to support youth outcomes.
